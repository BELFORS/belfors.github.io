If you're interested in becoming a member of BELFORS or would like to contribute to our mission of integrating forecasting for decision making, please reach out via email. We're particularly interested in hearing from:

  • Industry practitioners working with forecasting methods
  • Organizations looking for forecasting expertise or collaboration
  • Students interested in forecasting research
