Join Us

We welcome academics, researchers, and practitioners interested in forecasting to join BELFORS. Whether you're conducting cutting-edge research or applying forecasting methods to solve real-world problems, there's a place for you in our community.

If you're interested in becoming a member or learning more about membership opportunities, please get in touch.

Member Benefits

  • Access to networking events and workshops
  • Opportunities for collaboration between academia and industry
  • Sharing of best practices and research findings
  • Connection with Belgium's forecasting community
  • Participation in forecasting challenges and initiatives
