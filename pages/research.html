Research

Research Focus

BELFORS promotes research at the intersection of forecasting theory and practice. We encourage collaboration between academic researchers and industry practitioners to address real-world forecasting challenges.

Research Areas

  • Time series forecasting methodologies
  • Machine learning applications in forecasting
  • Demand forecasting and planning
  • Statistical methods and uncertainty quantification
  • Forecasting for decision support
  • Industry-specific forecasting applications
  • Forecast evaluation and improvement

Publications

Research publications and working papers from BELFORS members will be featured here. We aim to disseminate forecasting research relevant to both academic and practitioner audiences.

Collaborations

We actively seek collaborations between universities, research institutions, and industry partners. If you have a forecasting research project or challenge that could benefit from collaborative work, please reach out.
